# Competitor Analysis

## Toptal
- CTA: Hire Top Talent
- Trust: Strong vetting narrative
- Choice Reduction: Limited supply (Top 3%)
- Steal: Quality positioning
- Avoid: No pricing clarity

## Upwork
- CTA: Post a Job
- Trust: Ratings and job success score
- Choice Reduction: Filters and AI matching
- Steal: Structured onboarding
- Avoid: Decision fatigue due to too many options

## Clutch
- CTA: Find Companies
- Trust: Verified reviews and portfolios
- Choice Reduction: Category and location filters
- Steal: Authentic review focus
- Avoid: Static discovery flow
